Collapsible Sections

Can be used anywhere an 'a' tag is used to "expand" and "collapse" a hidden section somewhere on the page (they don't necessarily have to be co-located)

Hidden text highlighted like this for readability / understandability
Car Details , Click to Expand Section , Click to Collapse Section

Economy
Hertz
Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)
Pick up: Monday, July 8, 2013
Drop off: Tuesday, July 9, 2013

But it doesn't have to be hidden text that gets switched out.
You can alter the visible text like the example below:
All hotel reservations are prepaid. Your rewards account or credit card account will be charged when you complete your reservation.  View further details. Collapse details.
  • Hotel reservations are guaranteed for late arrival
  • Hotel reservations are generally ineligible for hotel award points or airline mileage
